SYMBOLS OF THE CITY OF ODESSA

City symbols of Odessa

1. The city symbols are the coat of arms, the flag, the city anthem, the mayor's standard, the description and order of use of which is determined by the Regulation on city symbols, which is approved by the city council.The anthem of Odesa is "Song about Odesa" from the operetta "White Acacia" to the music of I. Dunaevsky.

2. On the building of the city council, next to the State Flag of Ukraine, the flag of the city of Odessa is raised. An image of the coat of arms of the city of Odessa is placed near the entrance to the city council building.

3. Local self-government bodies, state authorities, associations of citizens, enterprises, institutions and organizations, officials, as well as individuals are obliged to treat city symbols with respect

Coat of the arms of the city of Odessa


FLAG OF THE CITY OF ODESSA

A rectangular panel with an aspect ratio of 2:3, divided into three equal vertical parts - red, white and golden yellow, on the white part - the coat of arms of the city of Odessa.
